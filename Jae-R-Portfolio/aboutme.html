About Jae Auden-Quinn Ruppelt

Hello! My name is Jae. Please do NOT call me Joy, nor Anna-May- that is my deadname and Aunt's/my legal middle deadname.


I am a queer (genderfluidflux they/them), disabled (Highly Empathetic/Sensitive Autistic, Bipolar II & ADHD- the middle and last are medicated well) Caucasian person. I love to be creative, and to design apps or websites for people or animals' well-being would be a dream come true! Seeing as I have photosensitive epilepsy (I have a Vegas Nerve Stimulator implant for it and I'm medicated for it, however), I am able to know what would be too much for those with that, since I used to have it unmedicated and I needed to use accessibility features specifically to remove flashing lights and bouncy animations.

I hope to design much for designs that are easily used by many, not just those who are abled!
